The Best Lasagna

A classic Italian dish, lasagna layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese between sheets of pasta. Often baked until golden and bubbly, this hearty dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 2 ½ teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 12 dry lasagna noodles
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
